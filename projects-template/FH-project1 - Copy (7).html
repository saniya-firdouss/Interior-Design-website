Project Overview

Modern Living Room Renovation

This living room renovation completely transformed a dark and outdated interior into a bright, elegant, and highly inviting family space. The redesigned layout features a custom curved sofa, soft neutral walls, decorative lighting niches, and vibrant accent colors that bring warmth and personality into the room. Large floral curtains and carefully selected décor elements create a cheerful and refreshing atmosphere while maximizing natural light throughout the space.

The renovation focused on improving both comfort and visual openness while maintaining a stylish contemporary aesthetic. Modern furnishings, layered textures, and clean interior detailing now give the room a more spacious and luxurious appearance. The transformation successfully turned a basic living area into a modern, cozy, and visually uplifting environment perfect for everyday family living.
